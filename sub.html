Walk the Path of Democracy with Rajpath

What does democracy look like up close?

What is Rajpath

Rajpath is an immersive program that helps students step outside textbooks and experience democracy in action. It takes you to the streets, institutions, and people who shape politics and governance, making democracy a living reality instead of a distant concept.

Our Motive

Our goal is to make young citizens see how decisions are made, how challenges are faced, and how leadership works on the ground. By connecting directly with communities and political processes, Rajpath builds a clear and relatable understanding of democracy.

Deeper Implications

Rajpath is more than a tour — it is a journey that sparks awareness, critical thinking, and responsibility. By living and observing the democratic process up close, students develop a deeper sense of citizenship and the confidence to become active contributors and leaders in shaping the nation’s future
